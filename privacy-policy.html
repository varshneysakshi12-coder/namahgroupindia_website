Privacy Policy .

Last Updated: March 2026

Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our users ("you"). This policy outlines how we collect, process, and safeguard your data across our diverse portfolio of Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) applications — including our campus recruitment training platform, Placement Pilot (pp.namahgroupindia.com) — digital platforms, and physical wellness lines, in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and applicable RBI guidelines.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Identity Data: Name, email address, physical address (for Seviorè deliveries), and verified KYC details.
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, and interaction metrics with our Generative AI platforms.
  • Financial Data: We utilize RBI-authorized third-party payment gateways (like Razorpay) for transaction processing. We do not store your full credit card numbers or raw banking credentials on our servers.
  • Placement Pilot — Assessment & Session Data: When you use the Placement Pilot platform, we may additionally collect:
    • Voice recordings and transcripts generated during AI-powered mock interview sessions.
    • Uploaded résumés, CVs, and cover letters submitted for AI-driven CV diagnostics.
    • Assessment scores, skill-mapping results, cognitive test performance, and adaptive feedback data.
    • Session metadata including duration, question-response patterns, and performance analytics.

2. Data Localisation

In strict compliance with Reserve Bank of India (RBI) regulations, all payment-related data and core personal data of Indian citizens processed by our systems are stored exclusively on servers located within the geographical boundaries of India.

3. AI & Data Processing

Across our digital platforms and SaaS applications, we utilize advanced conversational AI and proprietary diagnostic algorithms. By using these services, you consent to the processing of your voice, text, and other inputs solely to provide you with personalized, real-time feedback or core service functionality. We do not use your personal, identifiable session data to train our foundational models without your explicit opt-in.

Placement Pilot — AI Processing: The platform employs real-time voice intelligence for mock interview simulations, natural language processing for CV analysis, and adaptive algorithms for cognitive skill assessment. Your voice inputs are processed in real-time to generate interview feedback and are not stored beyond the active session unless you explicitly choose to save a session for review. CV documents are processed by our diagnostic engine to produce skill-gap reports and are retained only for the duration of your active account.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Authorized payment gateways for processing transactions.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies (like the RBI) when legally mandated.

5. Platform-Specific Disclosures: Placement Pilot

The following disclosures apply specifically to users of the Placement Pilot platform:

  • Data Retention: Assessment scores, skill profiles, and performance analytics are retained for the duration of your active account. Upon account deletion, all personal data — including session recordings, uploaded documents, and assessment history — is permanently purged within 90 days.
  • Session Recordings: Voice-based mock interview sessions may be temporarily recorded for the purpose of generating real-time feedback. These recordings are automatically deleted after feedback delivery unless you explicitly opt to save the session. Saved sessions are accessible only to you and are encrypted at rest.
  • Third-Party AI Services: Placement Pilot may leverage third-party AI infrastructure (such as large language model APIs) to power certain features. In such cases, only anonymized, non-identifiable data fragments are transmitted. No personal identity data, raw voice recordings, or full CV documents are shared with third-party AI providers.
  • Institutional Access: If you access Placement Pilot through a college or institutional partnership, your institution may receive aggregated, anonymized performance analytics. Individual session data, voice recordings, and personal documents are never shared with your institution without your explicit written consent.

6. Contact & Grievances

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact our Grievance Officer at grievance@namahgroupindia.com.
