About Us

About Namah .

Bridging biological luxury and synthetic intelligence.

Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd is an Indian diversified conglomerate operating at the intersection of deep technology and elite wellness. Headquartered in Bangalore, the technology capital of India, we are driven by a singular mission: to architect ecosystems that elevate human potential.

Our Divisions

Seviorè Wellness: Our luxury arm is dedicated to redefining botanical purity. We source the highest grade, therapeutic botanical actives globally to create bio-formulations grounded in dermatological science. Seviorè is about uncompromising quality, where nature meets the rigors of modern science.

Quantum & AI R&D: Our deep-tech division spearheads foundational quantum-based research while developing intelligent SaaS applications. We engineer scalable platforms that cater to the masses, adapting to diverse consumer and enterprise requirements.

Our Philosophy

Excellence is not an accident. Whether it is ensuring a 100% pure botanical extract or developing an artificial intelligence model that perfectly mimics human reasoning, our approach remains uniform: meticulous precision wrapped in effortless elegance.

Placement Pilot

The Visionary Behind Namah

Sakshi Varshney

Managing Director & CEO

Sakshi Varshney is the founder, Managing Director, and CEO of Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd. A visionary entrepreneur, she leads the company's strategic direction across its dual pillars of luxury wellness and deep technology.

Under her leadership, Namah Group launched Placement Pilot — an AI-powered campus placement preparation platform — and established Seviorè Wellness as a science-first botanical luxury brand. Her approach combines meticulous operational precision with a bold vision for leveraging technology to democratize access to quality and intelligence.
